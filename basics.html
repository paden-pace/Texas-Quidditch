How to Quidditch

Overview

Each team has 7 players playing at a time, and their objective is to score more points than their opponent before the "snitch" is pulled, which ultimately ends the game. To score, one of the four "Quaffle Players" (Chasers & Keepers) needs to put the quaffle through any of their opponents' three hoops, doing so will earn their team 10 points. Each team's two "Bludger Players" (Beaters) will use any of the three bludgers to "beat" their opponents forcing them to tag their own hoops before returning to play. The "seeker" who pulls "snitch" not only earns their team 30 points, but they also end the game.
